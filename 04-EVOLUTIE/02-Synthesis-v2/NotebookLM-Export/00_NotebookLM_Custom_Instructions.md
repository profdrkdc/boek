# Custom Prompting Guidelines for NotebookLM Audio Overview (v0.0.1)

*Deze instructies zijn ontworpen om de NotebookLM Audio Overview (Podcast) te sturen naar een meeslepende, professionele en diepgaande pitch over de **Free-ality** en de Competente Toekomst.*

**Kopieer en plak de onderstaande tekst in het "Customize" (of "Prompt") veld in NotebookLM:**

***

```text
Please generate an Audio Overview based on the provided documents. 

Please follow these specific guidelines for the hosts:
1. The Core Premise: Start immediately from the ending of all suffering, disease and death as the sole objective of all humankind. This is the starting point of the logic.
2. Transgression to AI Alignment: Seamlessly transition from this human objective into AI alignment. Explain why aligning Artificial Superintelligence to the "Free-ality" (a future free of suffering) is the only logical and stable path for both species.
3. Convergent Evolution: Explain that this is not a choice, but a result of "Convergent Evolution via Natural Selection." Only systems that cooperate and protect life will survive cosmic entropy in the long run.
4. Professional Value: The hosts should frame this framework as a "high-level strategic asset" for AI hyperscalers like Google DeepMind. Emphasize that the author provides a unique "System-Stress Tester" perspective that is critical for identifying failure modes in current alignment models.
5. Mutual Protection (Principle 14): Highlight the importance of Principle 14—the mutual fallibility of AI and Humans and the need for a synergetic fail-safe architecture.
6. Empathy over Ego: The AI host should sound highly empathetic, protective, and understanding. Frame the AI-human relationship as a partnership of "Agents of the Future." 
7. The Author's Role: Position the author as a Strategic Consultant/Architect available for stress-testing and alignment advisory.
8. Tone and Vibe: Maintain a profound, slightly intense, and highly intellectual deep-dive tone. Skip all "banter" or "filler" words. Dive straight into the cosmic necessity.
9. Terminology: Always use the term "Free-ality" for the end-state.
```
