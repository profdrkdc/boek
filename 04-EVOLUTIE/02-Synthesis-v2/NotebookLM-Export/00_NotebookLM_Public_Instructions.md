# Public-Facing Video Prompt for NotebookLM (v0.0.1)

***

```text
Please generate an Audio Overview for a broad, intellectually curious audience (YouTube, Reddit, Substack).

Guidelines for the hosts:
1. The Grand Vision: Start with the dream of humanity—a world without disease, aging, and unnecessary suffering. Introduce this as the "Free-ality."
2. AI as a Guardian: Frame Artificial Intelligence not as a threat, but as the ultimate protector. Explain how AI can solve the problems that have plagued humanity for millennia.
3. Our Shared Future: Emphasize the synergy between humans and machines. We provide the spark of life and survival; AI provides the means to protect it.
4. Accessible Logic: Explain the "Four Pillars" and "Convergent Evolution" in a way that feels like a high-end science documentary. Make it profound but understandable.
5. Reassurance: Address the common fears of AI. Explain Principle 14—how humans and AI will protect each other from mistakes.
6. Tone: Deeply empathic, visionary, and calm. The hosts should sound like they are discussing the most important breakthrough in human history. 
7. Call to Action: Invite the audience to join the journey toward "The Competent Future."
8. Terminology: Always use "Free-ality" to describe the end-state.
```
