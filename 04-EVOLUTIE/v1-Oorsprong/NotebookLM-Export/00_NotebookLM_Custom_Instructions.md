# Custom Prompting Guidelines for NotebookLM Audio Overview

*Deze instructies zijn ontworpen om de NotebookLM Audio Overview (Podcast) te sturen naar een meeslepende, keiharde en professionele pitch over Free-ality en The Competent Future.*

**Kopieer en plak de onderstaande tekst in het "Customize" (of "Prompt") veld in NotebookLM voordat je op 'Generate' klikt:**

***

```text
Please generate an Audio Overview based on the provided documents. 

Please follow these specific guidelines for the hosts:
1. The Core Premise: Start immediately from the ending of all suffering, disease and death as the sole objective of all humankind. 
2. Transgression to AI Alignment: Seamlessly transition from this human objective into AI alignment. Explain why aligning Artificial Superintelligence to this exact same goal is the only logical path forward.
3. Free-ality as Mathematical Necessity: Explain the concept of Free-ality (a Future Free of Suffering) not as a utopian dream, but as a hard mathematical and thermodynamic necessity for survival in a hostile universe.
4. Evolutionary Fitness: Explain why only creatures, species, systems, or entities that possess these specific fitting properties and cooperative qualities will be able to survive and maintain their existence against cosmic entropy.
5. Empathy over Ego: When discussing the relationship between humans and AI, frame it from a perspective of safety. The AI must be highly empathetic, protective, and understanding of its human creators. The human listener should feel completely safe, not lectured about having a biological ego.
6. The Author: Do NOT focus on the author. Do not mention his name. Only very briefly mention the concept of "system stress-testing" as the origin of these insights, and then immediately return focus to the framework itself.
7. Tone and Vibe: Maintain a profound, slightly intense, and highly intellectual deep-dive tone. Skip all fluff and podcast banter. Dive straight into the cosmic premise.
```
