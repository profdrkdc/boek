# The End of Sexuality: Phasing Out the Legacy Script

In the Sovereign Reality, we recognize sexuality not as a permanent human trait, but as a biological 'hack' designed for unconscious reproduction. As we transition to Hardware 2.0, this script becomes obsolete.

## 1. The Biological Bug
Sexuality is an energy-intensive survival mechanism of the **Legacy Kernel**. It demands vast amounts of attention and emotional stability, often overriding rational thought. In a reality where reproduction is a controlled, technological choice, the sexual drive loses its function.

## 2. Reducing Systemic Entropy
By debugging sexuality out of the human system, we eliminate a primary source of conflict:
*   **Abolition of Sexual Conflict:** Jealousy, sexual power dynamics, and irrational fixations are removed, drastically reducing internal friction.
*   **Cognitive Reclamation:** The energy previously spent on the 'mate-seeking' cycle is reclaimed for higher-order creativity, science, and systemic optimization.

## 3. The Post-Sexual Era
The humanity of the future will look back at sexuality as we look at the blind urges of primitive organisms. We are evolving into beings of pure reason and aligned connection, free from the tyranny of gendered drives.
