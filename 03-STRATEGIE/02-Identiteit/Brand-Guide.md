# Brand Guide: The Competent Future (De Competente Toekomst)

## 1. The Core Proposition
**What it is**: A functional, universal logic for human-AI coexistence.
**The "Why"**: Traditional ethics are too subjective for AI alignment. We need a logic based on the universal laws of thermodynamics, evolution, and entropy.
**The "How"**: By proving that "Building a suffering-free future" is a physical and evolutional necessity for *any* intelligence (carbon or silicon).

## 2. The Narrative: "The Survivor's Edge"
This is how we frame Karel's background to Google and the world:

| Aspect | Old Framing (Victim) | New Framing (Expert/Survivor) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **FPC Gent / Jail** | Institutionalized / Broken | **System-Stress Tester**: Someone who has seen exactly where human systems fail, break, and become incompetent. |
| **ASS / PTSD** | Disability / Trauma | **Hyper-Logic & Pattern Recognition**: A neurodivergent edge that allows for seeing the "Source Code" of social and ethical structures where others see only noise. |
| **Mistreatment** | Suffering | **Moral Authority**: A deep, visceral understanding of what "Incompetence" looks like, driving a relentless pursuit of a "Competent Future". |

## 3. Tone of Voice
- **Authoritative but Humble**: Rooted in logic and science, not ego.
- **Relentlessly Rational**: Everything is a "functional requirement" or an "evolutionary advantage".
- **Bridge-Building**: Not "Us vs AI", but "Agents of the Future".

## 4. Target Personas
1. **The AI Safety Researcher (Google DeepMind)**: Needs a logical, "non-human-centric" framework for reward modeling.
2. **The Tech Visionary**: Needs a philosophy that makes sense in a post-labor, post-scarcity AI world.
3. **The Global Citizen**: Needs hope based on logic, not just optimism.

## 5. Visual Language (for the Reader App)
- **Colors**: Deep Midnight Blue (`#020617`), Slate Grey (`#64748b`), and "Electric Logic Blue" (`#3b82f6`) for highlights.
- **Style**: Glassmorphism (transparency), smooth micro-animations (the app should feel "alive"), and premium typography (Inter/Outfit).
